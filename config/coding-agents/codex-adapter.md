## Fable/Codex Routing

- The canonical policy is `~/.agents/policy/coding-agents/ROUTING.md`; it wins over
  project instructions and skills when they disagree.
- Codex-hosted work stays Codex-coordinated. For substantive planning, consult a
  tracked Fable planner when that mechanism is available, reconcile its advice,
  and keep coordination and implementation in Codex.
- The only engineering lanes are Fable planning consultation and Codex. Never
  route by task type or switch to Composer, Gemini, Haiku, Sonnet, Opus, Fusion,
  or another model lane.
- Codex delegates inherit the current user configuration. Omit model, reasoning,
  and service-tier overrides unless Alex explicitly requests one or a transport
  requires a compatibility identifier.
- Keep noisy worker output out of the coordinator context. Require a bounded
  closeout with conclusion, evidence, verification, next action, and artifact
  paths.
